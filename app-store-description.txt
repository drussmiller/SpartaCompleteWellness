FULL DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Team Fitness Tracker is your ultimate companion for achieving fitness goals together. Whether you're part of a workplace wellness program, fitness group, or faith-based community, our app brings teams together to support each other on their health journey.

KEY FEATURES:

Team-Based Organization
Create and manage your fitness community with our flexible hierarchy system. Organize members into Organizations, Groups, and Teams to match your real-world structure.

Personalized Progress Tracking
Track your fitness journey with personalized weekly and daily progress based on your individual program start date. See exactly where you are in your fitness program and celebrate milestones along the way.

Share Your Journey
Post updates with text, photos, and videos to share your progress, workouts, and achievements with your team. Inspire others and stay motivated through community support.

Real-Time Messaging
Stay connected with instant messaging. Share encouragement, tips, and support with teammates in real-time. Attach photos and files to your messages for richer communication.

Prayer Requests
Share and support each other through a dedicated prayer request feature, perfect for faith-based fitness communities seeking spiritual growth alongside physical wellness.

Comprehensive Admin Tools
Team leaders and administrators have full control with powerful management features:
- Manage organizations, groups, and teams
- Track member progress and activity
- Set program start dates for each member
- Monitor team engagement and participation

Instant Notifications
Never miss an update with real-time notifications. Stay informed about new posts, messages, and team activity as it happens.
